About zealous bus services

Welcome to ZEALOUS BUS TRANSPORT, a specialist in bus passenger transport solutions. We operate a fleet of 141 buses, providing over 5.1 million passenger journeys and covering in excess of 7.5 million vehicle kilometers each year in regular government-subsidised contract transportation services in the Northwest and Gauteng Province in the Republic of South Africa, and employ a staff in excess of 300 employees.

ZEALOUS is a 99.85% black Empowerment Company established in 2002 to take advantage of the government initiated contract system and the privatization of Parastatal transport companies in terms of the National Land transport Transition Act22 of 2000.
